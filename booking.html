About Our Facility

CTU Daanbantayan Kalambuan Gymnatorium is a premier event venue designed to provide the best experience for your special occasions. With modern infrastructure, professional staff, and excellent service, we are committed to making your event unforgettable.

Our facility features spacious halls, state-of-the-art equipment, air conditioning, and ample parking. We cater to weddings, birthday parties, graduations, corporate events, and more.
